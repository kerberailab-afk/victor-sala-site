Why It Matters in NY & NJ

Local risks worth testing for

New York and New Jersey homes carry specific, well-documented risks. These inspections aren't upsells — they're protection.

OIL TANKS

$100k+ cleanup risk

Underground oil tanks appear in a large share of older regional homes. A leaking tank can trigger a six-figure environmental cleanup — a sweep finds it first.

RADON

#2 cause of lung cancer

Radon is a colorless, odorless gas linked to thousands of deaths a year. New Jersey has some of the highest-radon counties in the country. Testing is quick and simple.

DRONE & THERMAL

The new standard

Aerial roof and infrared inspections used to be premium extras. Today buyers expect them — and Victor has offered both for years.
