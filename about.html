Meet Your Inspector

30+ years building homes. Now he inspects them for you.

Victor Sala turned three decades in construction into one mission: helping buyers understand exactly what they're purchasing — with no fear, no fluff, and no surprises.

The Victor Sala Difference

A tradesman first, an inspector always

With 30+ years in the construction industry, Victor has dedicated himself to educating buyers about the importance of a quality inspection. That passion for excellence has made him the inspection agency of choice for many of the top brokers and real-estate agents in New York and New Jersey.

His hands-on education spans carpentry, HVAC, roofing, electrical, and plumbing — so when Victor looks at a home, he sees how it was actually built, and what's likely to matter down the road.

He complies with InterNACHI's Standards of Practice and Code of Ethics, and carries insurance for general liability, E&O, and negligent liability. His goal is simple: give every customer the best possible inspection at a great price — without ever being an alarmist.
