Specialty & Add-On Services

The inspections that save buyers six figures

Bundle these with your inspection in one visit. In NY & NJ, a few of these can be the difference between a smart purchase and a costly surprise.

Oil Tank Sweep

Detect buried tanks before you buy — a leaking tank can mean $100k+ in cleanup.

Radon Testing

The #2 cause of lung cancer. Colorless, odorless — and common across the region.

Sewer Scope

A camera down the main line finds root intrusion and breaks before they cost you.

Thermal Imaging

Infrared reveals hidden moisture, missing insulation and electrical hot spots.

Drone Roof Inspection

High-resolution aerial views of steep or unsafe roofs — no guesswork.

Wood-Destroying Insects

Termite & WDI reports — required by many lenders, essential in older homes.

Mold & Air Quality

Mold, lead-based paint and home-health testing for peace of mind.

Fireplace & Chimney

Plus pool & spa, energy audits, and pipe & cable locating on request.
